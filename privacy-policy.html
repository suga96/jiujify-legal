Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

Jiujify ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application designed to help Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu practitioners track their training progress, belt progression, and consistency. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. By using Jiujify, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and, optionally, your name and belt rank. This information is used to personalize your experience within the app.

Training Data. You may log training sessions, techniques, notes, and other BJJ-related data. This information is stored securely and is only accessible to you.

Device & Usage Data. We automatically collect basic technical data such as device type, operating system version, app version, and general usage patterns (e.g., session frequency). This helps us improve the app's performance and stability.

Subscription & Payment Data. If you subscribe to Jiujify Premium, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store), Google (Google Play), or Mercado Pago (for web subscriptions). We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card number, bank account details, or other financial information. We receive only a confirmation of your subscription status from these platforms via RevenueCat, our subscription management provider.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Jiujify app and its features
  • Personalize your experience (e.g., displaying your belt rank)
  • Manage your subscription and premium access
  • Improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Communicate important updates about the service

We do not use your personal data for advertising purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Jiujify is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Jiujify is operated from Chile. If you access the app from outside Chile, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries where our service providers operate. By using Jiujify, you consent to such transfers. We ensure that any data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

9. Compliance with Data Protection Laws

We are committed to complying with applicable data protection regulations wherever our users are located. This includes the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents, and Chile's Personal Data Protection Law (Law No. 19.628).

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. California residents may request disclosure of the categories of personal information collected and whether their data has been sold (we do not sell personal data).
